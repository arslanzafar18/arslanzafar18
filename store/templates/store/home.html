Legal Accessories Warehouse

Legal Accessories Warehouse is a leading international provider of court workwear and ceremonial clothing for professionals. We fit majorly members of the legal and academic cadre in the areas of daily court and occasional ceremonial wears for processions such as investitures and convocation ceremonies.

As it is the norm for robes to be worn during legal proceedings, call to bar ceremonies, certain legal and academic ceremonial occasions as well as legislative or parliamentary procession, we have developed over the years a strong ability in production of quality, standardized court and work wear for gentlemen of the bar and the bench in accordance with international practise and specifications.
